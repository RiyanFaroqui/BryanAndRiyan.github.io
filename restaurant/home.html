What is Bryan & Riyan's?

Welcome to Bryan & Riyan's, a culinary haven born from the shared vision of Bryan Parmar and Riyan Faroqui.

Inspired by their love for exceptional food and a desire to create a welcoming space, our restaurant is a testament to their passion for crafting memorable dining experiences.

What We Do

At Bryan & Riyan's, experience an exquisite fusion of flavors, where upscale dining meets culinary artistry. Our menu boasts expertly grilled premium cuts, complemented by a curated selection of crafted cocktails. From succulent steaks to tantalizing small plates, our cuisine is a celebration of refined tastes in a sophisticated setting.
