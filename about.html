What is Bryan & Riyan's?

Welcome to Bryan & Riyan's, a culinary haven born from the shared vision of Bryan Parmar and Riyan Faroqui.

Inspired by their love for exceptional food and a desire to create a welcoming space, our restaurant is a testament to their passion for crafting memorable dining experiences.

The History

Over the years, Bryan & Riyan's has evolved into a destination for connoisseurs seeking a harmonious blend of upscale dining and vibrant socializing. Our carefully curated menu, featuring modern twists on classic favorites, reflects the culinary journey that has unfolded within these walls.

Sourcing Our Ingredients

Dedicated to culinary excellence, we scour local markets for the freshest produce, collaborating with farmers who cultivate organic vegetables and herbs, ensuring a farm-to-table experience for our discerning guests. Quality is paramount in our kitchen, and our chefs work closely with trusted purveyors to source premium cuts of meat, fresh seafood, and artisanal cheeses, guaranteeing an unparalleled dining experience.
